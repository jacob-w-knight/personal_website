About Me

Welcome! I recently completed my PhD in the Department of Mathematics at Imperial College London, where I studied the physics of microscopic organisms using mathematical models and computational methods. Before that, I read Natural Sciences at Gonville and Caius College, Cambridge, graduating with First Class Honours and the BP Nevill Mott Prize for the top Theoretical Physics Masters project. Beyond research, I'm an experienced tutor, a trained mindfulness meditation teacher, and an enthusiastic data scientist -- feel free to explore the site and get in touch.
